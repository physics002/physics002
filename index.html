Breast Cancer Awareness

Join us in spreading awareness and supporting those affected by breast cancer.

Common Symptoms

  • Lump in the breast
  • Changes in breast shape or size
  • Skin dimpling
  • Nipple retraction
  • Discharge from the nipple

Myths & Misconceptions

Discover the truth behind common misconceptions about breast cancer.

  • Myth: Only women with a family history are at risk.
  • Myth: Breast cancer only affects older women.
  • Myth: Finding a lump always means cancer.
  • Myth: Mammograms expose you to dangerous radiation.

Did You Know?

Breast cancer is the most common cancer among women worldwide.

Survivor Story

Kimberley was just 24 when she found a lump. Despite initial concerns, she sought medical attention and was diagnosed with breast cancer. With treatment and determination, Kimberley is now cancer-free and encourages others to know their bodies.
